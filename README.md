# msba-portfolio-hongbo
# Business Problem
TruSource wants to reduce customer churn because losing customers decreases company's revenue and increases acquisition costs. Our group project predicts which factors will lead consumers to leave the comany, and so the company can prevent the problem early.
# Key results
Referral count is the important predictor of the customer churn.
Fiber optic is the important factor of customer churn risks.
Higher fees directly increase the probability of a customer leaving.

Model prediction is based on the past data, and the consumer behavior will change in the futuere. So some results maybe not be predicted by the model.
